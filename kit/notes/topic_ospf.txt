OSPF QUICK NOTES

Ką daro:
- Dinaminis routing protokolas
- Naudoja areas
- Dažniausiai basic reikia area 0

Basic:
router ospf 1
 network 10.0.0.0 0.0.0.255 area 0

Naudingos komandos:
show ip ospf neighbor
show ip ospf interface brief
show ip route ospf
show ip protocols

Troubleshooting:
- ar interface up/up?
- ar tas pats area?
- ar tas pats subnetas?
- ar nėra passive-interface?
- ar wildcard teisingas?
