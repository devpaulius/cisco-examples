ACL QUICK NOTES

Standard ACL:
- filtruoja tik source IP
Example:
access-list 1 permit 192.168.1.0 0.0.0.255

Extended ACL:
- filtruoja source, destination, protocol, port
Example:
access-list 100 permit tcp 192.168.1.0 0.0.0.255 any eq 80

Apply:
interface g0/0
 ip access-group 100 in

Atminti:
- implicit deny gale
- standard ACL dažnai arčiau destination
- extended ACL dažnai arčiau source
